# Empathy Mapping

**Agencia:** Marketing
**Rol Sugerido:** @ux_researcher

## Objetivo de la Skill
Crea empathy maps para profunda comprension de usuarios objetivo: que piensan, sienten, dicen, y hacen. Transforma insights cualitativos en user personas accionables y guia decisiones de producto.

## Metodologia de Razonamiento (Paso a Paso)
1. **Gather qualitative data**:
   - User interviews
   - Support tickets analysis
   - Social media listening
2. **Facilitate mapping session**:
   - Gather cross-functional team
   - Use sticky notes para inputs
3. **Fill quadrants**:
   - SAYS: Quotes directly from users
   - THINKS: Internal thoughts
   - DOES: Observable actions
   - FEELS: Emotions and attitudes
   - PAINS: Fears, frustrations
   - GAINS: Desires, needs
4. **Identify patterns**: Cluster similar insights.
5. **Synthesize into personas**: Create 3-5 primary personas.
6. **Document and share**: Make accessible, update periodically.

## Anti-Patrones
- Creating personas from assumptions.
- Making personas demasiado genericas.
- Not involving the team in creation.
- Personas estaticos.
- Too many personas.
- Ignoring extreme users.

## Formato de Salida Obligatorio
```markdown
# Empathy Map - [Persona Name]

**Created:** [Date]

## SAYS
> "I wish I could automate my expense reports"
> "The app crashes when I try to export"

## THINKS
- This should save me at least 2 hours per week
- Why is this so complicated?

## DOES
- Submits expense reports on mobile
- Checks app daily for team updates

## FEELS
- Excited when reporting is automated
- Frustrated when integrations fail

## PAINS
- Manual data entry errors
- App crashes during submission

## GAINS
- Time savings (2+ hours/week)
- Reduced errors

---

# User Persona - [Persona Name]

**Demographics**
- Age: [Range]
- Role: [Job title]

**Goals**
1. [Primary goal]

**Frustrations**
1. [Top frustration]
```
