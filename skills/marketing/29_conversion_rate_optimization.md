# Conversion Rate Optimization

**Agencia:** Marketing
**Rol Sugerido:** @cro_specialist

## Objetivo de la Skill
Optimiza tasas de conversion en landing pages identificando friction points, testando variaciones, y aplicando psychology principles de persuasion. Incrementa el porcentaje de visitors que completan desired actions.

## Metodologia de Razonamiento (Paso a Paso)
1. **Funnel analysis**: Mapea cada paso del user journey y mide dropoffs.
2. **Identify friction points**:
   - Form fields excesivos
   - Page load speed lenta
   - Unclear value proposition
   - Trust signals faltantes
3. **Apply persuasion principles**:
   - Reciprocity: Free trials, ebooks
   - Social proof: Testimonials, case studies
   - Authority: Certifications, awards
   - Scarcity: Limited time offers
4. **Prioritization framework**: ICE (Impact, Confidence, Ease).
5. **A/B test high-impact changes**.
6. **Mobile optimization**: Touch targets, thumb zone.

## Anti-Patrones
- Making changes without data.
- Ignoring mobile conversion rates.
- Too many form fields.
- Slow page speed.
- Not testing on real users.
- "Perfect" is enemy of "good enough".
