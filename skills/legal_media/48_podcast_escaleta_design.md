# Podcast Escaleta Design

**Agencia:** Media
**Rol Sugerido:** @content_producer

## Objetivo de la Skill
Crea escaletas (outlines) para podcasts que mantienen engagement, incluyen hooks efectivos, pacing apropiado, y CTAs estrategicamente placed. Estructura episodios que van from hook to payoff.

## Metodologia de Razonamiento (Paso a Paso)
1. **Episode structure basics**:
   - Hook (0:00-1:00): Grab attention immediately
   - Intro (1:00-2:00): Context, guest intro
   - Main content (2:00-40:00): Core discussion
   - Mid-roll ads (every 15-20 min)
   - Outro (last 3-5 min): Summary, CTA
2. **Hook strategies**:
   - Start with controversial take
   - Use cliffhanger teaser
   - Lead with shocking stat
   - Quote from upcoming content
3. **Segment design**:
   - Each segment = 1 thought unit
   - 5-10 min per segment
   - Clear transitions between segments
4. **Pacing variation**:
   - Mix of story, analysis, humor
   - Change energy levels intentionally
   - Build to climax
5. **CTA placement**:
   - Subscribe/follow: Mention twice (intro + outro)
   - Review request: Once per episode
   - Social engagement: Specific, actionable
   - Next episode teaser: End only

## Anti-Patrones
- Starting con generic "welcome to the show".
- No clear structure, rambling conversation.
- Too many topics, shallow coverage.
- Missing transitions between segments.
- Forgetting mid-roll ad placements.
- CTA too vague or too frequent.
