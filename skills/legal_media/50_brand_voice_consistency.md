# Brand Voice Consistency

**Agencia:** Media
**Rol Sugerido:** @brand_manager

## Objetivo de la Skill
Mantiene consistencia de voz de marca a traves de todos los canales y touchpoints, definiendo tono, vocabulario, messaging pillars, y arquetipos que guian todo el content creation.

## Metodologia de Razonamiento (Paso a Paso)
1. **Define brand archetype**:
   - The Innocent: Pure, optimistic
   - The Explorer: Curious, adventurous
   - The Sage: Wise, knowledgeable
   - The Hero: Brave, accomplished
   - The Outlaw: Rebellious, disruptive
   - The Magician: Transformative, visionary
   - The Lover: Passionate, connected
   - The Jester: Playful, fun
   - The Everyman: Relatable, grounded
   - The Caregiver: Caring, supportive
   - The Ruler: Authoritative, responsible
   - The Creator: Innovative, artistic
2. **Establish tone dimensions**:
   - Formal <-> Casual
   - Serious <-> Humorous
   - Reserved <-> Warm
   - Objective <-> Subjective
3. **Define vocabulary**:
   - Words to use consistently
   - Words to avoid
   - Industry terms to use sparingly
4. **Create voice principles**:
   - 3-5 core principles
   - Examples of do's and don'ts
   - Context-specific variations
5. **Document messaging pillars**:
   - Primary message (one core idea)
   - Supporting messages (2-3 key points)
   - How to handle objections
6. **Create training materials**:
   - Quick reference guide
   - Examples gallery
   - Content checklist

## Anti-Patrones
- Brand voice that changes with every new hire.
- Too many voice attributes, nothing sticks.
- Voice that doesn't fit actual audience.
- Being "professional" que significa nothing.
- Ignoring tone shifts for context.
- Brand voice as constraint sin flexibility.
